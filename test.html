Meet the Senior Team of Chartered Insurance Brokers

Matthew Hartigan | Managing Director

Matthew joined Higos in June 2018 and has over 26 years experience with some of the UKs leading brokers and insurers including Zurich and Royal Sun and Alliance, working in a number of different markets. Matthew is an Associate of the Chartered Insurance Institute and a Chartered Insurance Broker.

As expected: Matthew is responsible for all aspects of Higos, from our products to our people, ensuring we meet our Clients priorities and creating an environment where everyone in the Higos team develop and grow.

Different: When Matthew was 18 he turned down a contract as a professional footballer in favour of a career in insurance.

Trudy Brown | Product & Underwriting Director

Trudy joined Higos in 1997 and has over 28 years' experience in the insurance industry. She's ACII qualified and a Chartered Insurance Broker.

As expected: Trudy delivers and manages our schemes, facilities and insurer relationships as well as providing underwriting support.

Different: She was a child model, appearing in TV ads and magazines before she was even 5 years old. She says her friends would describe herself as being left-field and a little bit crazy.

Sarah Neale | Central Trading Director

Sarah joined Higos in July 1994. Shes ACII qualified and a chartered insurance broker has previously worked in both our personal lines and commercial lines divisions.

As expected: Sarah runs our Central Region (which includes our Somerton and Bridgwater Offices) as well as heading up our Claims and Agency teams.

Different: Less than 0.5% of all UK dog owners show their dog at Crufts - and Sarah is one of them. Shes also a massive fan of crazy golf.

Alan Clarke | Branch Trading Director

Alan joined Higos in July 2000 and has spent his whole working career with Higos carrying out both personal lines and commercial roles. Hes ACII qualified, was awarded as Highly Commended BIBA Young Broker 2015.

As expected: Alans responsible for managing the Higos Branch Network and the Branch Management Team, travelling between our 12 offices in the South West.

Different: Hes also dab hand at skittles, regularly turning out for The Comebacks in Division 1 of the Street and District League.

Julian Hilton | Sales & Markets Director

Julian, who joined Higos in September 2022, has over 30 years experience in the Insurance Broking sector. His passion is winning new clients and supporting the professional development of his Higos team mates.

As expected: Julian is responsible for the delivery of the Higos new business income objective, plus the strategic relationships we hold with insurer partners.

Different: As a former Sale Rugby Union player, Julian is a keen sport fan especially football / Manchester City FC, Rugby, Cricket and Golf. He also completed the Venice to Rome cycle ride in 2016 to raise money for Parkinsons UK.

Vanessa Casey | Head of Operations

Vanessa is the most recent addition to the management team having joined Higos in February 2021, she has over 20 years experience in the insurance industry having spent the last 16 years with the Brightside group where she held a number of positions within the business, most recently head of contact centre specialising in Taxi/Minibus & Non Standard Home insurance.

As expected: Vanessa will be responsible for reviewing the operational processes, looking to streamline the journey for both customers and employees across the Higos group.

Different: Vanessa manages a womens football team who compete in the National Womens League, which is tier 4 on the football pyramid.

Chris Currie | Head of Claims

Chris is a recent addition to the Higos team, joining in August 2020 as our claims manager. With over 30 years experience in the insurance industry, most recently with Jelf/Marsh Commercial and has previously worked for large insurers such as Royal and Sun Alliance and RBS Insurance Services.

As expected: Chris heads up the Higos commercial and personal lines claims teams managing all aspects of the process and provision of exceptional client claims service.

Different: Prior to going grey, losing his hair and putting on a few pounds, Chris dashing good looks  enabled him to be a model in his younger years and he auditioned for the Clothes Show Models competition in 1989.

Kari Clarke | Conduct and Operations Support Manager

Kari joined Higos straight from college in 2006 and during her successful career with us has worked in a number of different commercial roles. Kari is ACII qualified with Chartered Insurance Broker status.

As expected: Kari manages this vital role in the business as it controls the oversight of our first line of governance defence and ensures that we are operating within the governance framework as set out by the GRP Group requirements. In addition, the role also oversees all Client complaints along with a host of other responsibilities.

Different: Shes also a keen Pole Fitness dancer and is learning aerial silks.
